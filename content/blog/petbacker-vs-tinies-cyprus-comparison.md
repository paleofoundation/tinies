If you are looking for pet care in Cyprus — or looking to offer pet care services — you have probably encountered [PetBacker](https://www.petbacker.com/). It is a Singapore-based pet care marketplace that has been active in Cyprus for several years, with over 500 provider listings on [PetBacker Cyprus](https://www.petbacker.com/cyprus).

Tinies is the local alternative: a Cyprus-based pet care platform built specifically for this market, with a mission to fund animal rescue through every booking.

This is an honest comparison. We will tell you what PetBacker does well, where Tinies is different, and let you decide which platform is right for you.

## The basics

| | PetBacker | Tinies |
|---|---|---|
| **Headquarters** | Singapore | Cyprus |
| **Active since** | 2013 | 2026 |
| **Cyprus providers** | 500+ (listed) | Growing (new platform) |
| **Services** | Walking, sitting, boarding, daycare, grooming, training | Walking, sitting, boarding, daycare, drop-ins |
| **Payment** | Through platform | Through platform ([Stripe](https://stripe.com/)) |
| **Languages** | English | English, Greek, Russian |

PetBacker has been operating longer and has more provider listings. That is a straightforward advantage of being established. Tinies is newer but growing, and every provider is identity-verified before appearing in search results.

## Commission and fees: the biggest difference

This is where the platforms diverge most significantly.

**PetBacker's fee structure:**
- Providers pay 15% to 25% commission on every booking
- New providers start at 25%
- The rate decreases as you complete more bookings: 20% after 6 bookings, 19% after 150, down to 15% after 3,001 bookings
- If PetBacker detects that a provider is also working on another platform, the commission increases to 40%
- Pet training has a fixed 35% commission

**Tinies' fee structure:**
- Pet owners pay a 12% service fee at checkout
- Providers keep 88% of every booking from day one
- No tiered rates — 12% whether it is your first booking or your thousandth
- No exclusivity requirement — providers are free to work on any platform
- No penalty for being on multiple platforms

Let us put this in real numbers. A EUR 25/night boarding booking:

| | PetBacker (new provider) | PetBacker (established) | Tinies |
|---|---|---|---|
| Commission | 25% = EUR 6.25 | 15% = EUR 3.75 | 12% = EUR 3.00 |
| Provider keeps | EUR 18.75 | EUR 21.25 | EUR 22.00 |
| Over 100 bookings | EUR 1,875 | EUR 2,125 | EUR 2,200 |

A new provider on PetBacker earns EUR 325 less over their first 100 bookings compared to Tinies. An established provider still earns EUR 75 less.

And the exclusivity penalty is significant. If you list on both PetBacker and Tinies simultaneously, PetBacker could increase your commission to 40% — meaning you would keep only EUR 15 from a EUR 25 booking. On Tinies, there is no such penalty.

## Where the commission goes

On PetBacker, the commission funds the Singapore-based company's operations, development, and profit.

On Tinies, 90% of the 12% commission goes directly to animal rescue organisations in Cyprus. Every booking generates a donation. The total amount donated, the charities funded, and the per-charity breakdown are all publicly visible on the [Tinies Giving page](/giving) — updated in real time, no login required.

This is not a marketing line. It is the structural purpose of the platform. Tinies was built to fund rescue.

## Features comparison

**Search and discovery**

Both platforms offer search by location and service type with map views. PetBacker has a larger selection of providers in Cyprus due to its longer history. Tinies offers more granular filtering options including cancellation policy, minimum rating, and holiday availability.

**Provider profiles**

PetBacker profiles include photos, bio, rates, and reviews. Tinies profiles include the same plus: GPS walk tracking data, certification badges from completed training courses, a giving impact statistic showing how much the provider's bookings have contributed to rescue, and a structured availability calendar.

**Booking and payment**

Both platforms process payments securely. PetBacker charges the provider; Tinies charges the owner a service fee. On Tinies, payment is only captured when the provider accepts the booking — your card is authorised but not charged until the provider confirms.

**Trust and safety**

PetBacker offers basic profile review. Tinies requires government ID verification for every provider, manual review by the operations team, and completion of a safety course before the profile goes live. Every booking is covered by the Tinies Guarantee: up to EUR 2,000 for veterinary costs and EUR 500 for property damage.

**Communication**

Both offer in-app messaging. Tinies adds GPS walk tracking (owners can watch their dog's walk in real time on a map), photo updates during bookings, and a structured meet-and-greet scheduling system.

**Adoption**

PetBacker does not offer adoption services. Tinies operates a full [adoption](/adopt) platform connecting rescue animals in Cyprus with homes across Cyprus and Europe (UK, Germany, Netherlands, Sweden, Austria, Switzerland), including end-to-end logistics coordination.

## Customer support

PetBacker provides support through its global team, based in Singapore. Response times can vary depending on timezone differences with Cyprus.

Tinies is based in Cyprus. Support is local, in the same timezone, and understands the specific context of the Cypriot market.

## What PetBacker does well

To be fair, PetBacker has several strengths:

- Established presence with 500+ providers listed in Cyprus
- Grooming and training categories (not yet available on Tinies)
- A proven track record of completed bookings
- International brand recognition

If you need a specific type of service that Tinies does not yet offer, or if you need access to a larger pool of providers right now, PetBacker is a reasonable choice.

## What Tinies does differently

Tinies is built on a fundamentally different premise. It is not a transaction platform that happens to involve pets. It is a mission-driven marketplace where every booking funds the rescue and care of animals in Cyprus.

- Lower commission from day one (12% vs 15-25%)
- No exclusivity penalties
- 90% of commission to animal rescue
- Full public transparency on donations
- Government ID verification for every provider
- Safety training and certification
- GPS walk tracking
- Meet-and-greet scheduling
- International adoption coordination
- Local support in Cyprus
- Three languages (English, Greek, Russian)

## The bottom line

If you are a pet owner in Cyprus, Tinies offers a lower effective cost (the 12% service fee is comparable to PetBacker's pricing to owners), stronger provider verification, and the knowledge that your booking funds animal rescue.

If you are a pet care provider, Tinies lets you keep more of your earnings from day one, does not penalise you for working on multiple platforms, and gives you professional tools (training, GPS tracking, certification) that help you build a reputation.

If you care about the stray animal crisis in Cyprus, Tinies is the only platform that structurally addresses it through every transaction.
