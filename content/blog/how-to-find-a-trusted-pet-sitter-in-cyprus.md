Leaving your dog or cat with someone new can feel daunting, especially if you have relied on friends and family in the past. Cyprus has passionate pet people, but until recently there was no single place to compare verified sitters, read real reviews, and pay securely. Here is how to choose someone you can trust.

## Start with identity and verification

A serious provider should be willing to verify who they are. On Tinies, providers complete ID verification before they can appear in search. That is a baseline: you are not meeting a stranger from an anonymous post in a Facebook group. Ask whether they carry insurance if they offer boarding in their home, and whether they have relevant experience with your species and breed.

## Read reviews like a local

Look for patterns across several reviews, not just five stars. Did other owners mention communication, punctuality, and how their pet behaved afterwards? A sitter who sends a quick photo update without being asked is often worth their weight in kibble. If reviews mention the same concern repeatedly, believe the pattern.

## Match services to your pet

Not every animal needs the same thing. A senior cat may only need a calm drop-in visit; a high-energy dog may need a walker who understands leash reactivity. Check cancellation policies before you book — life in Cyprus includes last-minute travel and family emergencies, and you should know where you stand financially.

## Meet before the big trip

If the platform or the sitter offers a meet-and-greet, take it. Ten minutes in person (or a video call for international owners coordinating care) prevents mismatched expectations. Watch how they interact with your pet: do they kneel to the animal’s level, let the pet approach first, and ask thoughtful questions about food and medication?

## Use secure booking and clear payment

Paying through a platform protects both sides. You get a record of what was agreed, and the provider knows the booking is real. Avoid anyone who insists on cash-only with no paper trail for a first booking.

Cyprus deserves the same standard of pet care you would expect in London or Berlin. When owners choose verified, reviewed providers, the whole island’s animals benefit — including the strays and rescues your booking helps support through Tinies Giving. **No matter the size**, your pet’s comfort comes first.
